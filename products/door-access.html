Door Access Products List

M06

The M06 device has a compact design suited for modern door access. Built with rfid card and password/pin capabilites, it provides a straightforward functionality without the use of logs/timekeeping.

X6

The X6 offers the most budget friendly door access using multiple inputs including fingerprint, rfid card, and password/pin. Still does not have logs/timekeeping.

SC700

The SC700 is fully tailored to only accept rfid cards, up to an absurd amount of 30,000 users. This is great for companies who requires their ID as a requirment to open.

F04

The F04 offers a sleek touchscreen design with fingerprint and rfid card functions. This is a great choice for gyms and membership businesses with its expiration feature.

SF200

The SF200, featuring a vertical design, has both fingerprint and rfid card functionalities, with pin / password support.

F22

The F22 has a small and compact build featuring a touch pad. Same as the SF200, it can handle fingerprint and rfid card inputs at an even more massive scale, at the cost of no pin / password support.

TF1700

Waterproof, the TF1700 is a robust device that can handle harsh outside weather. It can handle fingerprint, rfid card, and password/pin.

IFACE3

The iFace3 can accomodate three biometric data inputs from fingerprints, rfid cards, and face recognition. It has a similar compact design as the F22.

FA210

The FA210 has the same specifications of the MB460 with a different design. A special order can be placed to include a wifi option at a price.
