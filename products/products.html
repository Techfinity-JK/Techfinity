The LX50 is the most budget friendly, fingerprint only, biometric device that we can offer. Good for starting businesses or monitoring on multiple franchises.
TX628

The TX628 is techincally the same as LX50, but a lot quicker in handling a lot of data, plus more storage.
SC700

The SC700 is fully tailored to only accept massive amounts of rfid card data. This is great for businesseses/companies who requires their ID as attendance.
T8

The T8 is the most budget friendly device that offers both fingerprint and rfid card capabilites. It looks like an upgraded version of the LX50 and TX628 line.
FA1000

The FA1000 has a flat, compact, and modern design that offers rfid card and face recognition functionalities. This is the most budget friendly device for the face recognition line.
BK100

The BK100 offers fingerprint and face recognition for up to a thousand users.
FA110

The FA110 is our most popular device in our product list. It offers the same fingerprint and face capabilites as the BK100, but with wifi.
SF200

The SF200, featuring a vertical design, has both fingerprint and rfid card functionalities, with pin / password support.
F22

The F22 has a small and compact build featuring a touch pad. Same as the SF200, it can handle fingerprint and rfid card inputs at an even more massive scale, at the cost of no pin / password support.
